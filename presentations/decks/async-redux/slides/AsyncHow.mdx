<li>A plethora of different async middlewares and enhancers exists</li>
<li>Most notable ones:</li>
<li>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Redux Thunk</strong> (allows writing functions that interact with Redux's `dispatch` and `getState`), comes with Redux-Toolkit by default</li>
    <li><strong>Redux-Saga</strong> (more powerful and complex, based on generator functions)</li>
    <li><strong>Redux-Observable</strong> (more powerful and complex, based on observables and streams)</li>
    <li><strong>Redux-Loop</strong> (middleground between <strong>Thunks</strong> and more complex solutions; based on effects in <strong>Elm</strong>)</li>
  </ul>
</li>
